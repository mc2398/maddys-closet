My Pink Dress:
It's Pink!

Fabric and notions I used for this project:

  • Fabric- 2 yards Stratton Hibiscus Solid Organic Cotton Twill from Mood
  • My sewing machine- with a regular presser foot and a zipper foot
  • One spool all purpose hot pink thread
  • My SPARKLY fabric scissors
  • A rotary cutter and cutting mat
  • A giant clear ruler
  • Candles to weigh down my fabric while I cut it (fabric weights would definitely work better but here we are)
  • A hot pink invisible zipper
  • A bunch of pins
